# EXECUTIVE DASHBOARD STRATEGY

## PURPOSE

Provide executive operational visibility.

Dashboard must show:
- operational performance
- SLA performance
- delay risk
- operational bottleneck

---

# DASHBOARD PRINCIPLE

Dashboard consumes:
- Voyage execution ONLY

NOT:
- planning draft
- tentative schedule

---

# PRIMARY EXECUTIVE KPI

- Total Voyage
- Active Voyage
- Delayed Voyage
- Completed Voyage
- OTB %
- OTD %
- OTA %
- Delay %
- Avg Dwell
- Avg Sailing Days

---

# EXECUTIVE VIEW

## OPERATIONAL SUMMARY

- voyage status
- operational bottleneck
- SLA risk

---

## DELAY ANALYTICS

- delay trend
- shipping line delay
- branch delay
- ETA risk

---

## PERFORMANCE ANALYTICS

- branch performance
- shipping line performance
- operational SLA

---

# VISUALIZATION

- operational heatmap
- delay trend
- KPI trend
- SLA trend

---

# FINAL TARGET

Dashboard becomes:
- executive operational cockpit
