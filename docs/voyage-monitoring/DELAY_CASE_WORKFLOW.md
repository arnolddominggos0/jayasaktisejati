# DELAY CASE WORKFLOW

## PURPOSE

Define operational disruption handling.

Delay Case manages:
- ETA delay
- ETD revision
- vessel substitution
- operational escalation

---

# FLOW

Potential Delay
→ Monitoring Alert
→ Delay Review
→ Delay Case Opened
→ TAM Confirmation
→ Operational Approval
→ Resolution

---

# DELAY CASE OWNER

Delay Case belongs to:
- Voyage

NOT:
- VesselPlan

---

# DELAY CASE CONTENT

- chronology
- operational issue
- ETA revision
- ETD revision
- vessel substitution
- approval history
- escalation history

---

# ESCALATION LEVEL

LOW
MEDIUM
HIGH
CRITICAL

---

# VESSEL SUBSTITUTION FLOW

Issue Detected
→ Alternative Voyage
→ TAM Review
→ Approval
→ Shipment Reassignment

---

# FINAL TARGET

Delay Case becomes:
- operational incident workflow
