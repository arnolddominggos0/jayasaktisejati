## Ringkasan produk

Platform operasional logistik laut & distribusi untuk mengelola shipment, voyage/schedule, resource lapangan, monitoring KPI, dan kontrol akses berbasis role + branch/depot scope.

## Scope MVP

1. Auth + RBAC + branch/depot scoping.
2. Master data (user, branch, depot, customer, vessel, voyage, port).
3. Shipment lifecycle + tracking + dokumen (waybill/resi/packing list).
4. Planning schedule/voyage + vessel plan.
5. Dashboard monitoring KPI operasional.
